Picking A Level

Choose between Easy, Medium, or Hard. Go easy-breezy or dive right into haunting challenges.

To pick a level, simply hover your mouse over any of the levels below and witness the magic!

Playing A Level

To begin playing a level, you must hover your cursor over the magnifying glass, which reveals a room that is filled with different items.

You will count till 5 seconds to view the objects in the room while keeping the cursor on the image.

Once 5 seconds are up, you must then drag the cursor outside of the image, keeping you're cursor in the blue box.

Now, you will have to spot the differences between the two images!
